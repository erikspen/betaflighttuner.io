Betaflight Rates Tuner

An alternative approach for setting rates in Betaflight.

ROLL
Slope*: Max deg/s:
PITCH
Slope: Max deg/s: Shape:

CONFIGURATOR PARAMS

RC Rate: Super Rate: RC Expo: OTX Expo:
ROLL
Super Rate: OTX Expo:
PITCH
RC Rate: Super Rate: RC Expo: OTX Expo:
YAW

Notes

-- Slope sets the rate-increase at mid-stick. It is equivalent to RC Rate when RC Expo is zero.
-- Shape determines how the rate curve grows versus stick command. Shape=0 is equivalent to using zero Super Rate. Shape=1 is equivalent to using zero RC Expo.
-- OTX Expo refers to the expo curve on an Open TX-based tranmitter. Most will set it to zero, but it's included here as it can extend rate curve possibilities.
-- Positive OTX Expo is discouraged due to loss of resolution near mid-stick.
-- Negative OTX Expo can increase resolution near mid-stick, grow the rate curve faster, and allows Roll & Pitch to have different initial slopes.


* - Giving Pitch and Roll different Slope values requires use of OTX Expo.
