Parameter help

  • Slope represents center-stick sensitivity. Tells you how quickly commanded deg/s raises as you first deflect the sticks. Scaled to be equivalent to RC Rate.
  • Shape interpolates between a curve based on Super Rate, and a curve based on Expo. Conviently, Slope and Max deg/s are not affected while adjusting.
  • OpenTX Expo refers to the expo curve on an Open TX-based tranmitter. Typically zero.. Included here as negative OpenTX Expo has a few uses.
  • If Super Rate value is red in color, a negative OpenTX Expo is causing a non-increasing slope versus stick position and Super Rate should be increased.
