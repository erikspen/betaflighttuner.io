Parameter help

  • Slope represents center-stick sensitivity. The value is scaled such that it's equivalent to RC Rate when no Expo is present.
  • Shape interpolates between a curve based on Super Rate, and a curve based on Expo. While adjusting, Slope and Max deg/s are unaffected.
  • OpenTX Expo refers to the expo curve on an Open TX-based tranmitter--typically zero.. Negative OpenTX Expo adds a few possibilities for adjust curve shape.
  • If Super Rate value is red in color, a negative OpenTX Expo is causing a non-increasing slope. To resolve, Super Rate should be increased.
  • The Raceflight<->Betaflight conversions offered above all try preserving center-stick feel to varying degrees. If you notice a better fit for one of the Slope++/-- options, then an improved overall fit may be available when constraints on matching center-stick shape are relaxed; see links below for additional Raceflight<->Betaflight conversions.
